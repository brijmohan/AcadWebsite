Women Scientists in NLP

ICON 2016 Workshop

Call for Participation

Poster-presentation by young women scientists

We invite women NLP scientists working both in NLP research departments and industry to submit their ongoing research work in the form of a detailed 2-page abstract. Papers selected through a peer-reviewed process will be invited to present their work in the form of a poster presentation.

In the same poster session, we will also urge participation from linguists working on language and gender.

Abstract submission deadline : November 1
Abstract acceptance notification : November 15
