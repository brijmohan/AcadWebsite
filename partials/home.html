I am a Masters Research student at IIIT-H under Dr. Manish Shrivastava. My research interests include Speech & Language Technologies. Currently, I am working in the area of Spoken Dialog Systems in mobile/handheld devices. The goal of my project is to build a Dialog System Framework for mobile devices which is multi-linguistic and easily domain-adaptable (especially for healthcare in rural population of India). This work will eventually lead to the development of a Virtual Assistant for Indian Languages (ITRA, Media Lab Asia).
